pixelator

I don't even know how to explain this project. It surged as an inspiration from gui dávid's personal website. I guess there's just something about minimalist art, like pixel art, that fascinates me. It might the room for imagination that exists in a work that doesn't feel complete yet. in fact, this might be the exact reason why I like da Vinci's first fresco, The Battle of Anghiari , so much: it feels shallow, yet, it tells you so much about what's going on--you can tell every expression depicted in the scene converges to resemble struggle; no one is saving a single calorie in The Battle.
